Knowledge and Experience

I have 20 years experience in the field and have worked with a wide range of clientele groups and service outlets. I have a D.E.C in special care counselling and a masters in social work. I am a member of my professional order (OTSTCFQ). My experience includes but is not limited to domestic violence, depression and anxiety, parenting and couples counselling. I have worked with the elderly, refugees, children with physical and behavioural challenges as well as youth at risk. I am passionate about wellness and self care and believe it should be a daily practice. In my work, I employ a solutions focused goal oriented approach. In addition, I use cognitive behavioural therapy and emotion focused therapy techniques in my counselling. I also use motivational interviewing to help clients find the best path for themselves. I am attentive to providing a safe and confidential space for change and am always welcoming of people of all backgrounds, genders and orientations.
